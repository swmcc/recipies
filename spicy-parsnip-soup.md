# Spicy Parsnip Soup

## Ingredients

	Shit load of parnsips
	An onion
	Sprinkle of garlic powder
	750Ml of water
	2 veggie stock cube 
	40ml of milk
	Chilli
	1 Tin of tomatoes

## Directions

Fry the parnsips and onion. Whack in the tomatoes add the stock and boil for 15mins. Simmer for 5 and add the milk. Blend it all up.
